Privacy Policy

Prismix Image Converter is designed to feel trustworthy and simple: you choose an image, Prismix processes the current request, and your browser receives the result. The platform is created by Hakkı Burak Bakırcı.

Uploaded files

Uploaded files are processed in server memory for the active conversion request. Prismix does not create a public media library, user profile, account area or permanent storage for uploaded images in this project build.

Local previews

Browser previews use temporary object URLs so you can see selected files and crop areas. Refreshing or leaving the page clears the current browser-side preview state.

Cookies and local storage

Prismix uses necessary local storage to remember your cookie choice. Optional analytics and advertising preferences are separated from necessary storage and should only be activated according to your consent and local legal requirements.

Advertising transparency

Ad areas are separated from primary upload, convert and download controls. This helps keep the tool usable and supports a safer AdSense-ready layout.
